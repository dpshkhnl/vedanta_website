About us

Pewa Saving and Credit Cooperative Limited (PEWA) in short is a legitimate multipurpose cooperative organization. It is formally registered with the Division Sahakari Office of the Government of Nepal and Sahakari Ain 2048 and has obtained license from concerned authorities to operate its business from Nepal. Its office is located at the heart of Chitwan, Tandi. PEWA is established in Nepal by a group of enthusiast and experienced Bankers, Corporate Individuals and entrepreneurs for providing its service to the business community and other people residing in its periphery, who wants their hard earned money to be safe and earn better revenue.
